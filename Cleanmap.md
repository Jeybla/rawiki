Syntax
------

-   **[cleanarea](/cleanarea "wikilink")** "<map name>",<x1>,<y1>,<x2>,<y2>;
-   **cleanmap** "<map name>";

Description
-----------

These commands will clear all items lying on the ground on the specified map, either within the x1/y1-x2/y2 rectangle or across the entire map.
