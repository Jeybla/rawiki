Syntax
------

-   [rentitem](/rentitem "wikilink") <item ID>,<duration>;

Description
-----------

Gives you an item that lasts for <duration> seconds.

Example
-------

To rent out a red potion for 7 days you would use the following
***rentitem 501,604800;***
